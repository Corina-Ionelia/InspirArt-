Original Artworks

Series 1: Identity

The works presented here bring together a selection of original pieces by Xenia Botea, drawn from series and lines of inquiry that define her artistic practice.

At their core lies a sustained investigation of affective registers and processes of identity transformation, articulated through chromatic dynamics and a carefully developed pictorial language.

The visual vocabulary integrates echoes of geometric abstraction and expressionism, shaping images that engage symbolically with themes such as identity, maternity, childhood, inner balance, and the tensions of everyday existence.

Additional works, including large-scale pieces or works belonging to distinct corpora, may be available upon request.

Series 2: Interior Narratives

This series explores the studio and the domestic interior as spaces of reflection and symbolic construction. Figuration is situated within spatial frameworks shaped by light and compositional tension, where artistic practice, maternity, and self-representation coexist.

Geometric interventions and the relationship between the body and interior space shape images in which observation and introspection intersect, and the act of painting becomes part of a broader narrative about presence, care, and identity.
