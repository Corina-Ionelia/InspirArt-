ABOUT ME

My practice is rooted in a figurative-geometric language, while remaining open to formal and conceptual diversification.

My works employ layered symbolism, at times explicit and at times subtly encoded, inviting multiple readings.

Each composition is conceived as a vehicle for meaning, extending beyond its purely visual dimension.
