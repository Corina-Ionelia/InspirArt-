WELCOME TO

MY ARTISTIC

Xenia BOTEA

I'm Xenia, a full-time artist living and working in Romania. Art has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. I have always painted, drawn, and created.

At the age of 12, I made a promise to myself: to become an artist. This dream has been a constant and guiding force in my life.

I work in series, using mixed media on various surfaces, and I enjoy experimenting. Art, the natural world, and human psychology remain my greatest passions.

For over 20 years I have had the privilege of combining them in what I call "the best job in the world."

My passion for art and for people drives me to organize workshops and creative sessions for all ages: wine & paint sessions for adults, art activities in schools, and workshops for children and parents.

Warmly,
Xenia
